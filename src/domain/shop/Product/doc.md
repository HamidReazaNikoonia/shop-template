## Endpoints


> __GET__  Get all Products of all users and all shopChanel `/product`
> __GET__ Get all Products of spesific user `/:user_id/product`

> __POST__ create the product `/:user_id/product`
> __PUT__ update the product `/:user_id/product`
> __DELETE__ delete product `/:user_id/product`
